Corona Isolation Management System

Welcome to the Corona Isolation Management System. Are you sensing, that you have any symptoms related to COVID-19. If you sense that you have some symptoms, kindly fill in your details in the next page. Once you enter into the centre, you will be tested for Corona, if its a positive result, you will be immediately transferred to a nearby hospital. Whereas, if the result is negative, you have to remain in the isolation centre for 14 days, for the symptoms to dissapear, and reduce your chance of being affected by corona.
